About

CanPred is a simple classifier using a radial kernal SVM, which classifies paitients into different kind of cancer tyoe from whole genome RNAseq data.

Currently our classifier only supports 5 classes of cancers.

  • PRAD: Prostate Adenocarcinoma
  • LUAD: Lung Adenocarcinoma
  • BRCA: Breast Cancer
  • KIRC: Kidney Renal Clear Cell Carcinoma
  • COAD: Colon Adenocarcinoma

For detailed information on the model please go through documentation page
